# Premium Features

The premium version of the Monte Carlo Retirement Simulator includes advanced capabilities for comprehensive financial planning. These features are available in the paid version.

## Dual Income / Spouse Modeling

- Per-person 6-bucket portfolio (Traditional, Roth, Taxable for each spouse)
- Independent asset allocation, RMD schedules, and withdrawal ordering per person
- Spouse income streams: pension, SERP, salary overlap periods
- Household survival scoring across both lifetimes
- Per-person portfolio balance tracking and reporting

## Stress Testing

- **Fragility Window** — Apply worst-case historical returns during the critical early retirement years to stress-test sequence-of-returns risk
- **Historical Scenario Overlays** — Replay specific market environments:
  - 1970s Stagflation
  - 2000 Dot-Com Crash
  - 2008 Global Financial Crisis
  - 1990 Gulf War Recession
  - 2022 Rate Shock
- Configurable overlay modes: replacement, floor, or blend

## Tax Optimization

- Roth conversion ladder with year-by-year optimization
- Configurable Traditional vs. Roth contribution split
- Net Investment Income Tax (NIIT / 3.8% surtax)
- FICA modeling for earned income
- Dividend and interest tax drag analysis

## Advanced Spending

- Multi-phase spending profiles (Go-Go / Slow-Go / No-Go retirement phases)
- Pre-retirement per-year spending overrides
- Income offset mode — reduce withdrawals by earned income

## Enhanced Reporting

- Per-person portfolio breakdown tables
- Individual RMD schedules
- Roth conversion ladder tables
- Tax treatment breakdown (ordinary vs. capital gains vs. tax-free)
- Income source breakdown with audit trail
